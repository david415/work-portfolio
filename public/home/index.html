About Me

tl,dr; curriculum vitae/résumé available upon request

I am an independent security researcher, public speaker and software developer. If you are reading this website it is likely that you are looking for a Golang or Rust software developer, software architect, or chief technical officer. You are in the right place.

I started my career as a network tools developer and operations engineer. I worked for Internet service providers and software companies, automating systems and network operations. The banality of DevOps no longer interested me and shortly after the Edward Snowden document leaks I transitioned my career into more of a software development role and took a keen interest in computer security, especially cryptography and anonymous communication networks.

I did a lot of public speaking while working on the Katzenpost mix network project. If you are researching my background I suggest you watch one of these videos to get a sense for how I present complex technical topics.

The work presented in this portfolio has everything to do with my personal interests and basically nothing to do with the proprietary software I’ve written for commercial companies. Therefore there are certain aspects of my experience that are not emphasized herein. This is but a small sample of my work experience. To understand more fully my work experience and history you should ask me for a copy of my curriculum vitae/résumé.
